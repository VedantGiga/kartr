Analytics Features

Our comprehensive analytics dashboard provides you with valuable insights about your YouTube channel performance:

Channel Performance
  • Subscriber Growth - Track your audience growth over time
  • View Trends - Monitor view count patterns and peaks
  • Watch Time Analysis - See how much time viewers spend on your content
  • Revenue Tracking - Keep track of your monetization performance
Audience Insights
  • Demographic Breakdown - Age, gender, and location data
  • Viewing Habits - When your audience watches your content
  • Engagement Metrics - Likes, comments, and share statistics
  • Audience Retention - How long viewers stay watching your videos
In the full application, you'll have access to real-time data and interactive charts that allow you to filter by date ranges and specific metrics. The analytics dashboard is updated hourly to provide you with the most current information about your channel's performance.
